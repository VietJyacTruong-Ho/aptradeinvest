Dashboard with trade data and investment annoucements from asia pacific countries - Indiana
https://vietjyactruong-ho.github.io/aptradeinvest/

Four tiers of sources: 
1. Structured census-style directories from JETRO and KOTRA
2. Official announcements from the Indiana Economic Development Corporation (IEDC) and the Governor's office
3. News and commercial databases including the Indianapolis Business Journal and Area Development
4. Ongoing monitoring via Google Alerts and targeted press release extraction.

Each record captures the company, location, sector, investment type, announced dollar value, jobs, and year.

Coverage is strongest for Japanese and South Korean investments above $5M, where IEDC press releases and official directories (JETRO and KOTRA) provide high-confidence sourcing.

All dollar values and job figures reflect ONLY publicly announced commitments and/or intentions, actual expenditures are not verified.
